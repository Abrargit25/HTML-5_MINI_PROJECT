FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I book a flight?

It's easy! Simply use our flight search form to enter your departure and arrival destinations, choose your travel dates, select the number of passengers, and hit the 'Search Flights' button.

Q: Can I change my booking?

Yes, you can. Please log in to your account and navigate to the 'My Bookings' section to manage your booking details.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards for secure online payments. You can also choose from various payment options during the checkout process.

Q: Are my personal details safe?

Absolutely. We prioritize the security and privacy of your personal information. Our website uses advanced encryption technology to safeguard your data.

Q: How can I contact customer support?

If you need assistance, you can reach out to us at support@flighthub.com. Our dedicated support team is available to help you.
